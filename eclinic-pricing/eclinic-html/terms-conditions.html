E-Sushrut@Clinic is a SaaS-Based, smart, digital solution for Clinics, Nursing Homes, Pharmacies & Labs (“Health Facilities”), and is owned and introduced by C-DAC (hereinafter referred to as “Us”, “Ours”, “Ourselves”, “We”).

The E-Sushrut@Clinic is designed, with Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (“ABDM”) to support the integrated digital health infrastructure of the country by bridging the existing gap amongst different stakeholders of Healthcare ecosystem through digital highways. In alignment with the mission and vision of the ABDM, the E-Sushrut Clinic solution provides a state of the art digitized workflow for various Health Facilities so as to provide and enhanced patient experience:

  • The Doctors to consult, write prescriptions within linking of pharmacies, labs, IPD and save patient records online and authentically; and save patient records online and authentically; and
  • The Patients to get access to our Registration, Billing, Pharmacy, In-Patient services with various related alerts and interactive reports.

The E-Sushrut@Clinic solution is a feature-rich, user-friendly online platform that provides superior performance and dependability in managing the end-to-end operations of aHealth Facility for its effective and efficient operations. It is speciallydesigned to meet the clinical and non-clinical (administrative, operational, and financial) needs of a Health Facility.
