About Us

We aim to serve as a Launchpad for
entrepreneurs and innovators

It is funded by MSInS (Maharashtra State Innovation Society) and hosted by MUHS (Maharashtra University of Health Sciences) Nashik. Established with a vision to create a hub for healthcare innovation, research, and entrepreneurship to foster the development of cutting-edge solutions that enhance healthcare delivery and improve the well-being of communities.

We are a dedicated incubation center focused on empowering startups in the healthcare sector and advancing the integration of artificial intelligence in healthcare. Our mission is to drive transformative change by nurturing groundbreaking ideas, facilitating interdisciplinary collaboration, and providing resources to bring innovative healthcare solutions to life.

Our Impact

Create sustainable platform that fosters growth, learning and global partnerships in healthcare innovation

  • Incubate healthcare start-ups, supporting them through their early stages of development.
  • Facilitate funding for start-ups incubated at the centre.
  • Collaborate on research projects with incubated start-ups to promote healthcare innovation.
  • Forge partnerships with healthcare institutions and organizations to foster interdisciplinary collaboration.
  • Assist start-ups in successfully entering the healthcare market.
  • Regularly assess and report on the impact of start-ups on healthcare sector.
