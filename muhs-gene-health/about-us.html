GeneHealth Laboratory was established under the visionary leadership of Lt. Gen. Dr. Madhuri Kanitkar (Retd.), Hon’ble Vice Chancellor of MUHS,
with the goal of delivering cutting-edge, low-cost genetic testing to patients across the state.

Empowering Genetic Health for Every Family in Maharashtra

Our Journey

  • Nov 2022

    Lab launched

  • 2023-2024

    Established Sickle Cell and Thalassemia screening, cancer mutation testing (EGFR, BCRABL, BRCA1/2), and karyotyping and worked out the cold chain work flow for transporting samples all over Maharashtra.

  • 2025

    Rollout of newborn screening for congenital hypothyroidism, antenatal screening for thalassemia and sickle cells, rare gene disorder testing (like SMA or DMD), FISH testing and more.

  • Upcoming

    Expanding the newborn screen to other metabolic disorders and SCID, Genomics in NICU, pre-natal diagnosis and more.

Our team brings together clinical geneticists, molecular biologists, pathologists, pediatricians and trained genetic counsellors dedicated to delivering quality, compassion, and accuracy in testing.
Comprehensive, Affordable Genetic Testing

Services

    Newborn Screening

  • Congenital Hypothyroidism (TSH)
  • Congenital Adrenal Hyperplasia (CAH) – UPCOMING
  • Severe Combined Immunodeficiency (SCID via TREC assay) - UPCOMING

    Antenatal Screening

  • Hemoglobinopathies: Thalassemia & Sickle Cell Disease
  • Spinal Muscular Atrophy (SMA)
  • Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD)

    Pediatric & General Genetics

  • Fragile X syndrome
  • Hearing Loss (GJB2 / GJB6 Connexin gene)
  • Cytogenetics: Karyotyping & FISH (e.g., Down syndrome, Turner syndrome

    Cancer Genetics

  • BRCA1/2
  • RT PCR testing of BCR-ABL for CML
  • RT PCR testing EGFR mutation for Lung Cancer
