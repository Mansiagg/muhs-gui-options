Know About MUHS GeneHealth

GeneHealth Laboratory was established under the visionary leadership of Lt. Gen. Dr. Madhuri Kanitkar (Retd.), Hon’ble Vice Chancellor of MUHS, with the goal of delivering cutting-edge, low-cost genetic testing to patients across the state.

The GeneHealth Laboratory , established under MUHS at the Pune Regional Centre, serves as the academic and service extension of the GIBN Department. This molecular and genetic diagnostic laboratory provides a real-time training environment for students, fellows, and researchers.

Together, the GIBN Department and GeneHealth Lab offer a unique, integrated model of education, diagnostics, research, and outreach, committed to building India’s genomic healthcare ecosystem from the ground up.
