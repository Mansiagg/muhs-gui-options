FPLMIS is a Web based
Supply Chain Management System

It is a major step towards adapting technology to improve Supply Chain of the Family Planning Commodities across the Country. FP Division uses this Application to deals with the procurement, storage and supply of Family Planning Commodities required by public health facilities/units across the country, under the Ministry.

FPLMIS It is a digital platform designed to streamline and optimize the processes involved in the Procurement and distribution of Family Planning Products.
Here's an outline of its key components and functionalities:

Demand Module

  • Demand Configuration FP Division
  • Demand Filling State Only
  • Demand Approve by State Authority State Approval Authority
  • Demand Compilation FP Division
  • Demand Approve by FP FP Division
  • Demand Verify By FP FP Division
