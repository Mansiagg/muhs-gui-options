MAHARASHTRA UNIVERSITY OF HEALTH SCIENCES (MUHS), NASHIK

The state Government of Maharashtra has established and incorporated an independent University of the health Sciences for the purposes of ensuring proper and systematic instruction, teaching, training and research in modern medicine and Indian system of medicine in the State of Maharashtra and to have balanced growth in the medical sciences so also in uniformity in various courses in medical faculty in the State, and to provide for matters connected therewith and incidental thereto.

Dissemination, creation, preservation of knowledge and understanding by teaching, research, extension and service and by effective demonstration and influence of its corporate life on society, in general, with specific objectsas provided in the MUHS Act, 1998.
As per MUHS Act 1998, u/s 4, objectives of the University are,
  1. to carry out its responsibility of creation, preservation and dissemination of knowledge.
  2. to promote discipline and the spirit of intellectual inquiry and to dedicate itself as a fearless academic community to the sustained pursuit of excellence.
  3. to encourage individuality and diversity within a climate or tolerance and mutual understanding.
  4. to promote freedom secularism, equality and social justice as enshrined in the Constitution of India and to be catalyst in socio-economic transformation by promoting basic attitudes and values of essence to national development.
  5. to extend the benefits of knowledge and skills for development of individuals and society by associating the University closely with local and regional problems of development.
  6. to carry out social responsibility as an informed and objective critic, to identify and cultivate talent, to train the right kind of leadership in all walks of life and to help younger generation to develop right attitudes interests and values.

Brief Biodata of Shri Ramesh Bais
Chancellor
Governor of Maharashtra

Shri Ramesh Bais was sworn in as the Governor of Maharashtra on 18 February 2023. Shri Bais is a respected name in the politics of the erstwhile state of Madhya Pradesh and the present Chhattisgarh as well as in national politics.

Shri Bais has more than five decades of experience in parliamentary politics, social work and organization work. He has held various responsibilities in public life ranging from Councilor to Union Minister of State and Governor.

Born on August 2, 1947 in Raipur (Chhattisgarh), Mr. Ramesh Bais was educated in Raipur.

In 1978, he was elected as councilor in Raipur Municipal Corporation for the first time. He was an elected member of the Madhya Pradesh Legislative Council from 1980 to 1985. In the year 1989, Shri Bais was elected to the Lok Sabha for the first time from Raipur. He went on to set the record of getting elected to Lok Sabha from Raipur for seven consecutive terms.

In the year 1998, Shri Ramesh Bais was appointed as the Minister of State in the Ministry of Steel and Mines in the cabinet of Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee.

During the period from 1999 to 2004, he served first as the Minister of State for Chemicals and Fertilizers and then as the Minister of State for Information and Broadcasting.

In the year 2003 Shri Ramesh Bais was appointed as the Minister of State (Independent Charge) in the Union Ministry of Mines and as the Minister of State (Independent Charge) in the Union Ministry of Environment and Forests.

From 2009 to 2014, Mr. Ramesh Bais was the Chief Whip of the Bharatiya Janata Party in the Lok Sabha. During the period from 2014 to 2019, when he was a member of the 16th Lok Sabha, he was the Chairman of the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Social Justice and Empowerment. Shri Bais has done extensive work on the issue of rights of persons with disabilities as well as transgender persons.
