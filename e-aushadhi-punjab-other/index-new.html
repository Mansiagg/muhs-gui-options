About e-Aushadhi

Supply of Quality Medicine Transparent Drug Procurement Procedure Upto Date Medicine Inventory.

DVDMS (Drugs and Vaccine Distribution Management System) is a software platform to automate various activities of Directorate General Medical Health, Govt. Of Punjab. It comprises of Drug and Vaccine Supply Chain Management that deals with Purchase Order, Inventory Management & Distribution of various drugs etc. Also it helps for managing receipt, Issue, Quality Control, vaccines and other health sector goods that are supplied to States under various disease control programmes.

Our Services

e-Aushadhi is a web based application which deals with the management of stocks of various drugs, sutures and surgical items required by different district drug warehouses of different states. The prime objective of a District Drug Warehouse is to supply drugs to the various medical institutes that are associated with the given district drug warehouse.

A Platform to automate various activities of Punjab Medical Services and Infrastructure Corporation Limited

Storage and Supply

Procurement, storage and supply of medicines, surgicalitems, medical equipment and other medical supplies/stores.

Organised Distribution

Receipt, storage and distribution of supplies of drugs and allied stores including various vaccines, received from various international bodies.

Life Saving Drugs

Meeting the emergency requirements of life saving drugs and other allied items in the country, arising out of natural calamities.
