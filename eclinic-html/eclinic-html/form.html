ABOUT E-SUSHRUT CLINIC

In a visionary move towards digital health transformation, C-DAC has recently introduced a Software as a Service (SaaS) based solution. This forward-thinking initiative is tailored to serve the unique needs of small clinics, nursing homes, pharmacies, and laboratories. By providing an affordable, accessible, and scalable digital platform, the SaaS-based solution empowers these healthcare providers to access state-of-the-art software and services. This cost-effective approach not only boosts operational efficiency but also enhances the overall quality of patient care. C-DAC's commitment to advancing healthcare technology continues to make a profound impact on healthcare delivery nationwide, facilitating better and more accessible healthcare services for all.
